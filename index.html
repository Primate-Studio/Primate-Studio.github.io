About Us

Who We Are

Primate Studio is a passionate collective of 5 game developers dedicated to creating experimental and boundary-pushing gaming experiences. Inspired by projects like "The Asylum of Creation," we believe in games as an art form that can challenge, surprise, and engage players in unique ways.

Our journey began with a shared vision during our first game jam, where we discovered the magic of transforming creative ideas into interactive experiences. Since then, we've been refining our craft and developing our distinctive style.

We embrace the experimental nature of game development, where each project is an opportunity to explore new mechanics, narratives, and visual styles that push the medium forward.
